✦ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Is QuizCraft AI free to use?▼
Yes! Creating an account and generating quizzes is completely free. You can upload any PDF and generate up to 5 questions per quiz on the free plan. Paid plans for higher limits are coming soon.
What types of questions does it generate?▼
QuizCraft AI generates 4 types automatically: Multiple Choice, True/False, Fill in the Blank, and Short Answer. The AI selects the best mix based on your document content.
How accurate are the generated questions?▼
Because we use Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), questions are grounded in your actual document rather than general model knowledge. This significantly reduces hallucinations and keeps questions relevant.
Is my PDF data stored or shared?▼
No. Your PDF is processed entirely in memory during your session and is never written to disk or shared with third parties. Your documents remain yours.
What is the maximum PDF size I can upload?▼
You can upload PDFs of any size. For best results, we recommend setting a page range of 5–20 pages. You can quiz different page ranges of the same PDF multiple times.
Can I retake the same quiz?▼
Yes! After viewing your results, click "Retake Quiz" to attempt the same question set again — perfect for spaced repetition. Or upload a new PDF for a completely fresh quiz.
Why is Google sign-in not working yet?▼
Google and GitHub OAuth is actively being developed and coming very soon. In the meantime, creating a free account with email and password takes under 30 seconds — same great experience.
What AI model powers QuizCraft?▼
QuizCraft uses Google Gemini 2.5 Flash Lite for question generation and Gemini Embedding 001 for document indexing. FAISS is used as the vector database for fast similarity search.
